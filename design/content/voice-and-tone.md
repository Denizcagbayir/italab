## Voice and tone — ITAlab

**Audience:** Turkish speakers learning Italian (A1–A2). UI language is Turkish; content examples are Italian.

### Voice

- Encouraging, clear, adult — not childish gamification spam.
- Short imperative CTAs: `Devam et`, `Başla`, `Kontrol et`, `Sonraki`.
- Errors: calm + specific (“Doğru cevap: *sono*”) — no blame.

### Tone by context

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Lesson / exercise | Focused, one instruction at a time |
| Correct | Brief positive (`Doğru`, `Bravo`) |
| Wrong | Neutral correction + next action |
| Settings / data | Plain and precise |

### Label consistency

- Use `Yol` for the course map, not `Müfredat` in nav.
- Use `Pratik` for free practice hub.
- Prefer `Seri` / `XP` as established stats language.
