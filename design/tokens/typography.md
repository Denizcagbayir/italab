## Typography tokens — ITAlab

| Role | Spec | Use |
|------|------|-----|
| Display / brand | `Fraunces` (`--font-display`), ~1.35–2.4rem, weight 700 | Logo, hero, unit titles |
| Body | `Outfit` (`--font-body`), 16px base, line-height ~1.5 | UI, exercises, nav |
| Heading M | Outfit/Fraunces 1.15–1.35rem, semibold | Section titles |
| Body small | 14px | Meta, hints |
| Caption | 12–13px | Badges, timestamps |

**Rules:**
- One primary page title per view.
- Italian examples may use display font sparingly; Turkish UI stays body font.
- Avoid ad-hoc sizes; stick to the scale above.
